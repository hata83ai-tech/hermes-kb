# Weekly Synthesis

End-of-week reflection for patterns, progress, and planning.

## Structure

### 1. The Week at a Glance
Days worked / major time blocks.

### 2. Accomplishments
Major wins and completed projects.

### 3. Challenges Faced
What was difficult and why.

### 4. Patterns Noticed
- When did you do your best work?
- What drained your energy?

### 5. Key Learnings
Biggest insights from the week.

### 6. Next Week
Top 5 priorities.

## Monthly Version

Every 4th weekly synthesis, add:
- Monthly theme assessment
- Major milestone review
- Quarter progress check
