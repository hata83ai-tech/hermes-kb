# De-AI-ify Text

Remove AI-generated patterns and restore natural human voice.

## What to Remove

### Overused Transitions
- "Moreover," "Furthermore," "Additionally"
- Excessive "However"

### AI Clichés
- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "Let's dive deep"
- "Harness the power of"

### Hedging Language
- "It's important to note"
- "It's worth mentioning"

### Corporate Buzzwords
- "utilize" → "use"
- "facilitate" → "help"
- "optimize" → "improve"
- "leverage" → "use"

### Robotic Patterns
- Rhetorical questions followed by immediate answers
- Obsessive parallel structures
- Announcement of emphasis

## What to Add

- Varied sentence lengths
- Conversational tone
- Direct statements
- Specific examples

## Example

**Before (AI):**
> In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, leveraging AI effectively isn't just about utilizing cutting-edge technology—it's about harnessing its transformative potential.

**After (Human):**
> AI works best when you use it for specific tasks. Focus on what it does well.
