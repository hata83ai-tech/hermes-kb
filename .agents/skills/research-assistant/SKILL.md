# Research Assistant

Conduct thorough research on topics by searching and synthesizing findings.

## Process

1. **Initial Search** - Search vault for the topic
2. **Deep Dive** - Read all relevant notes, extract insights
3. **Synthesis** - Create summary with patterns and gaps

## Output Structure

```markdown
# Research Summary: [Topic]

## Existing Knowledge
- What's already in the vault

## Key Themes
1. Theme 1
   - Supporting notes: [[note1]], [[note2]]
2. Theme 2

## Contradictions/Tensions
- Where ideas conflict

## Gaps
- What's missing

## Recommended Next Steps
1. Specific research needed
2. Questions to explore
```

## Tips

- Cast a wide net initially, then focus
- Look for surprising connections
- Don't ignore contradictions - they're where insights live
- Suggest concrete next actions
